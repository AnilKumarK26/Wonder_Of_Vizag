Deomali - Jewel of the Eastern Ghats

Discover the Majestic Deomali Peak

Deomali stands as the highest mountain peak in the Odisha-Andhra Pradesh border region, offering an unparalleled experience for nature lovers, adventure enthusiasts, and trekkers. Located in the Eastern Ghats, this magnificent destination promises breathtaking views and thrilling experiences.

Peak Highlights

Rising to an impressive altitude, Deomali provides a stunning panoramic view of the surrounding landscape. The peak is part of the Eastern Ghats mountain range, characterized by its lush greenery, diverse flora and fauna, and spectacular geographical formations.

Paragliding Adventure

Deomali has emerged as a premier destination for paragliding enthusiasts. The unique topography and wind conditions make it an ideal location for both novice and experienced paragliders. Visitors can experience the exhilaration of soaring above the mountains, capturing breathtaking aerial views of the Eastern Ghats.

Key Attractions

  1. Viewpoints: Multiple vantage points offer stunning 360-degree views of the surrounding valleys, forests, and distant landscapes. These viewpoints are particularly magical during sunrise and sunset.
  2. Trekking Routes: The mountain offers various trekking trails suitable for different skill levels. These routes wind through dense forests, offering opportunities to explore the rich biodiversity of the region.
  3. Paragliding Zones: Dedicated areas for paragliding with professional instructors and necessary safety equipment. Visitors can enjoy tandem flights or take lessons if they're interested in learning.
  4. Nature Trails: Explore the rich ecosystem of the Eastern Ghats, with opportunities to observe local wildlife, unique plant species, and breathtaking natural landscapes.

Adventure Activities

Beyond paragliding, Deomali offers various adventure activities:

  • Trekking and hiking
  • Nature photography
  • Bird watching
  • Camping
  • Wildlife observation

Best Time to Visit

The ideal time to visit Deomali is between October and March. During these months, the weather is pleasant, with clear skies and comfortable temperatures. The post-monsoon and winter seasons offer the best conditions for outdoor activities and paragliding.

Practical Information

Visitors should:

  • Carry appropriate trekking and outdoor gear
  • Check weather conditions before planning activities
  • Hire local guides for safe and informative experiences
  • Respect local ecology and follow sustainable tourism practices
